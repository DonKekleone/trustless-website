Abstract

Trustless Payments is an XRPL-native escrow framework that replaces subjective trust with deterministic code. Funds are locked, conditions are monitored, and value is released only when pre-agreed signals are met.

Philosophy

“A payment fails if trust breaks.” We level the playing field by binding promises to verifiable conditions—removing room for opportunism while preserving speed and simplicity.

Mechanics

Users define the escrow amount, destination, target conditions, and expiry. Funding is detected on-ledger. Upon satisfaction or expiry, the system automates release or return. A 5.89% fee is applied: 50% to the developer wallet and 50% is used for buy-and-burn of the associated token.

Token Alignment

Fees drive scarcity via automated buy-and-burn. Utility NFTs may further reduce fees and expand governance rights.

Roadmap

Milestones include broader condition types, extended analytics, and cross-chain adapters—while keeping the core mantra: simple, verifiable, trustless.
