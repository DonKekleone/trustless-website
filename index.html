How it works

1. Create

Open the bot and enter: escrow amount, recipient, contract address, target market cap (whole number), and expiry in days.

2. Fund

Send the exact XRP to the escrow address with the provided destination tag. Post your TX hash. The bot auto-detects funding.

3. Automate

If goals are hit before the deadline, funds release. Otherwise, they refund. No middlemen. No drama.
