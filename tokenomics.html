Mechanics We Implement

A. Locked Liquidity

  • Seed with ~15k XRP + 10% supply; lock proof published.
  • No internal LP expansion bucket; organic POL may form later from earned fees.

B. Dynamic Fee Burn

  • Base fee 5.89% (NFT holders: 1%).
  • Dynamic burn: burn share scales with on-chain volume bands (posted monthly).
  • 50% of fee to dev wallet rNdipHhkQdgrc46TJTGeABZrQuES2QZfaM; 50% buy+burn $XRK.

C. Staking & Usage Rewards

  • Funded from the Community (10%) bucket.
  • Rewards tied to verified bot usage (avoid idle inflation).
  • NFT-holder boosts respected; parameters published per epoch.

D. Milestone-Gated Unlocks

  • Marketing & Dev tranches release only when adoption KPIs are hit.
  • All unlocks pre-announced with reason codes and tx hashes.
  • Emergency pause (multi-sig) with public incident report.
