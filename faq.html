Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about how Trustless works, safety, fees, refunds, token/NFT utilities, and where to get help.

Is this financial advice?

No. Trustless is a utility for automated escrow on XRPL. Nothing here is investment advice or a promise of returns.

Do I share my secret keys with the bot?

Never. You fund escrows from your own wallet. The bot does not ask for or store secrets.

How do fees work?

Escrows include a 5.89% fee. Split: 50% to the dev wallet for continued development; 50% buys and burns the associated token—fully on-chain.

What happens when goals are not met?

Funds automatically refund to the original sender after expiry if objective conditions aren’t satisfied.

Do I need a trustline to trade?

Some tokens on XRPL require a trustline before trading. If/when it’s required for $TRUSTLESS, we’ll show the issuer and limit in the buy guide.

Is the bot open-source?

Core escrow logic is kept lean and auditable. We’ll publish more interfaces and monitors over time. Follow the roadmap for updates.

Where can I read the whitepaper?

Right here: Whitepaper (PDF + HTML).

What utility do the NFTs provide?

Fee reductions, governance and/or fee share (for select collections), private updates and early experiments. See NFTs for details.

How do I buy $TRUSTLESS?

Follow the 4-step guide: install Xaman, create a safe wallet, fund with XRP, and trade on XRPL DEX. See How to Buy.

Where can I get help?

Ping us on Telegram and we’ll walk you through it: Open Telegram.
